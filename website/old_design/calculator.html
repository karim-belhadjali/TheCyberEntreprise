Guide
What is a token calculator?

A token calculator allows a community member or holder of a token to make a quick prediction of what their holding would be at a targeted market cap or token price.

How to use a token calculator?

The user will input both the native token working inside the Cyber Enterprise ecosystem, as well as any fiat currency they would like to see their evaulatuted holdings in.

Why is a token calculator important?

The token calculator is important so that any holder has the ability to evaluate his current holding at various intervals, and ease them into creating a reasonable entry and exit strategy. Both entry and exit strategies pose as one of the most daunting tasks for any investor. At the Cyber Enterprise we cannot stress the importance of these enough, as having emotions dictate your decision making skills at the heat of the moment is a fault many succumb to. Ensuring our investors maintain level headedness and have the proper tools in doing so is something dear and close to us.
